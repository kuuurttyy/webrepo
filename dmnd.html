A2 Product Design Coursework

For my A2 Product Design coursework, I chose to designed and built a 1:20 scale model of a shop following a brief and client specifications. The project consisted with initial designs drawn by hand, development of the designs, then choosing a final design to construct into my model. For the development and final design, I made each component in Google SketchUp at the same scale I would be building at to get a better visual guide of how the thickness of materials, and the spacing between the components would look when translated into a physical medium.

I also designed a new logo in Illustrator styled on the brands original logo to create a separate yet still recoginasable identity for the store. The logo was used on the front of the model as well as on a printed flyer to promote the grand opening of the store if it were real.

Convex

For a group project in my first year at university, we were tasked with idealising a futuristic design-fiction style company, complete with logo design and videos to demonstrate the products the company makes.

The idea for Convex was based around a smart contact lens which gave the user a heads-up display as well as genetically improving their ability at tasks such as cooking, music and sports.

We produced a series of videos with the aim to advertise the benefits of the product, and then a website for them to go on which also provided more information about Convex.
